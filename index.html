SmartInventory
Demand-Driven Inventory Analytics

QuickMart was losing revenue every week to preventable stockouts — shelves running empty on weekends and seasonal peaks because a fixed reorder schedule ignored actual sales velocity. I built an end-to-end analytics system that analyzed 8,100 sales records across 90 products, identified weekend demand surges of 1.2x–2.5x baseline, and delivered dynamic reorder recommendations that replace schedule-based restocking with data-driven decisions. Delivered with an interactive Streamlit dashboard, a business memo, and a stakeholder presentation.

Stack: Python · SQLAlchemy · SQLite · Streamlit · pandas · Faker

Produce Shrink Analysis
Retail Inventory Simulation

90-day produce department shrink simulation built on domain knowledge from working in grocery retail. Identified that Berries and Leafy Greens drive 61% of total shrink loss and that Monday is consistently the highest waste day — driven by weekend overstock. Delivered with 3 visualizations and data-driven reorder recommendations.
